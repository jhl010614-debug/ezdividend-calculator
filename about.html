About This Calculator

A free tool for projecting long-term wealth through dividends, DRIP, and leveraged investing.

What Is This Tool?

The Smart Dividend, DRIP & Leverage Wealth Calculator is a free, browser-based financial projection tool. It helps investors model how their portfolio might grow over time by combining capital appreciation, dividend income, and the power of compounding through automatic reinvestment (DRIP).

All calculations run entirely in your browser — no data is ever sent to a server, and no account is required.

Key Features

  • Model any combination of annual return rate and dividend yield
  • Toggle DRIP (Dividend Reinvestment Plan) on or off to compare outcomes
  • One-click presets for popular funds: VOO, QQQ, SCHD, JEPI, JEPQ, and leveraged ETFs (SSO, UPRO, QLD, TQQQ)
  • Add monthly contributions to simulate dollar-cost averaging
  • Instant results: total balance, total invested, annual and monthly dividend income
